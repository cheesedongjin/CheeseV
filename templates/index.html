CheeseV What's CheeseV?

CheeseV started as a typo — I accidentally typed a “V” after cheese, my favorite food.

Instead of fixing it, I kept it. To me, it’s a reminder that small mistakes can lead to something meaningful.

I don’t love cheese because it's the best-tasting food. I just like it — and that's enough.
